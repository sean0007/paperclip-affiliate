1. Freqtrade

★★★★★ 4.8/5 · Open Source · Self-Hosted
💰 Free (self-hosted)

Freqtrade is the gold standard for DIY crypto traders. It's an open-source Python trading bot that gives you complete control over every parameter. Strategies are written in Python, backtesting is built in, and you can run it on a $10/month VPS or your own machine.

What We Liked

  • Completely free, no subscriptions, no platform fees
  • Full backtesting with historical data before you risk a cent
  • Extremely customizable — write your own strategies or use community ones
  • Runs on Binance, Bybit, OKX, and 20+ other exchanges
  • Active Discord community with 10k+ members sharing strategies
  • Dry-run mode works perfectly — tested extensively with our own desk
  • Requires technical setup (Python, Docker, API keys) — not zero-code
  • No hosted/cloud option — you manage the infrastructure
  • Strategy quality varies wildly — bad strategy = bad results

Our Testing Results

Ran TrendFollowingBase strategy on BTC/USDT 4H timeframe in dry-run for 4 weeks. Profit factor: 1.10 across 178 trades. Max drawdown: 18%. Solid baseline, not a money-printer.

Best For

Traders who want full control, enjoy tweaking, and have even basic Python skills. Also great if you want to run many strategies simultaneously across multiple pairs.

Visit Freqtrade → Self-hosted · Free

2. Binance Trading Bots

★★★★☆ 4.6/5 · Built-in to Binance
💰 Free to use (spread-based fees)

Binance has quietly built one of the most user-friendly bot ecosystems into its exchange. Grid trading, DCA (Dollar Cost Averaging), and signal bots are all available directly from the Binance app or website — no third-party tools needed.

What We Liked

  • Already on Binance? Bots are right there, no sign-up needed
  • Grid bots work exceptionally well in ranging markets
  • DCA bot is dead simple for long-term accumulation
  • Native USDT-M and COIN-M futures bots
  • Mobile app support for monitoring on the go
  • Only works on Binance — not portable to other exchanges
  • Strategy customization is limited vs. Freqtrade
  • Spread fees can eat into small accounts

Best For

Beginners who already have a Binance account and want to dip into automation without learning code or managing third-party services.

Try Binance Bots → Exchange account required · Free to activate

3. Pionex

★★★★☆ 4.3/5 · Exchange + Bots Built-in
💰 Free built-in bots · 0.05% per trade fee

Pionex is both an exchange AND a bot platform. They offer free built-in trading bots including Grid, DCA, and arbitrage bots. The major advantage: you don't need to connect to external services — everything runs inside Pionex.

What We Liked

  • Zero additional cost for bot features — already included in exchange
  • Grid Bot and DCA Bot are genuinely easy to activate in 2 clicks
  • US residents can use it (many bot platforms block US users)
  • Arbitrage bot exploits price differences between exchanges automatically
  • Mobile app works well for monitoring
  • Smaller exchange than Binance — lower liquidity on some pairs
  • Bot strategies are not customizable
  • Trading fees slightly higher than Binance spot

Best For

US-based traders or anyone who wants the absolute simplest path to running a trading bot with zero configuration.

Try Pionex → Sign up free · Built-in bots

4. 3Commas

★★★★☆ 4.4/5 · Third-Party Bot Platform
💰 $29–$99/month (14-day free trial)

3Commas is a third-party bot management platform that connects to your Binance, Bybit, FTX (deprecated), and other exchange accounts. It offers DCA bots, grid bots, and trailing features that aren't native to most exchanges.

What We Liked

  • Multi-exchange management in one dashboard
  • DCA (Dollar Cost Averaging) bots are excellent for recovering from losses
  • Smart Trade terminal for manual entries with bot-assisted exits
  • Good variety of pre-built strategies
  • Signals marketplace — copy strategies from top traders
  • Subscription cost adds up — $29/month minimum
  • Setup requires connecting API keys (security review needed)
  • Platform has had some outages historically

Best For

Traders who want sophisticated DCA strategies and are willing to pay for convenience. Best value at the $49/month plan.

Try 3Commas → $29+/month · 14-day free trial

5. Cornix

★★★★☆ 4.1/5 · Telegram-Native Trading Bot
💰 $24.99–$89.99/month

Cornix is a Telegram-based trading bot that focuses on signal trading. Connect it to your exchange via API, subscribe to signal channels, and the bot executes trades automatically when signals fire. Great for traders who follow crypto Twitter or paid signal groups.

What We Liked

  • Direct Telegram integration — familiar interface for most crypto traders
  • Auto-copy signals from trading groups or channels
  • Backtesting available for strategy refinement
  • Supports Binance, Bybit, Bitmex, Deribit
  • Monthly subscription — expensive for what it does
  • Signal quality depends entirely on the channels you follow
  • Telegram-first — less polished web UI

Best For

Traders who already follow signal providers on Telegram and want to automate following those signals without manual execution.

Try Cornix → $24.99+/month
