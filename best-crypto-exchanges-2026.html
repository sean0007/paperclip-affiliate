3. Coinbase

★★★★☆ 4.5/5
Maker 0.4% · Taker 0.6% (standard)

Coinbase is the most regulated exchange in the US and the easiest entry point for newcomers to crypto. It's publicly traded (NASDAQ: COIN), FDIC-insured for USD balances, and trusted by millions. Higher fees than competitors but unmatched for ease of use and compliance.

Pros

  • Most trusted exchange for US users
  • FDIC insured USD balances up to $250k
  • Easiest onboarding — credit card, bank transfer, Apple Pay
  • Publicly traded company with regulatory oversight
  • Coinbase Pro has lower fees for active traders

Cons

  • Highest fees among major exchanges
  • Limited automation options vs. Binance/Bybit
  • Fewer altcoin pairs

Automation Support

⭐⭐⭐ Good. Recurring buys for DCA, Coinbase Pro API available but less bot-friendly than offshore exchanges.

4. Kraken

★★★★☆ 4.4/5
Maker 0.16% · Taker 0.26%

Kraken is one of the oldest US-friendly exchanges, known for its rigorous security audits and transparent operations. It's a favorite among European traders and those prioritizing regulatory compliance. Good spot trading with decent liquidity.

Pros

  • One of the most secure exchanges (never hacked)
  • Strong regulatory compliance in EU and US
  • Good selection of fiat pairs (EUR, USD, GBP, JPY)
  • Staking available for 20+ coins
  • OTC desk for large trades

Cons

  • Higher fees than Binance/Bybit
  • UI is dated compared to newer competitors
  • Slower to list new altcoins

Automation Support

⭐⭐⭐⭐ Good. Full REST and WebSocket API. Kraken Pro has advanced order types. Good for bot trading.
